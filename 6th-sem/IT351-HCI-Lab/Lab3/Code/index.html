Steps

  • In the next page, you will be shown a list of animals.
  • Try to remember the animals mentally.
  • You will have only 10 seconds to look at the list of animals and remember them, after which the page will automatically change.
  • A timer will indicate the remaining time.
  • When you are ready, click on the START button
  • Once started, you will not be able to return.

Remaining Time: 10 seconds

    Instructions

    • From the given list of animals, identify the animals which were in the list previously shown to you and memorized by you.
    • Select the animals by clicking on them.
    • If you wish to deselect them, you can click on the respective animal again.
    • As only 8 animals were shown, you don't need to select more than 8 animals. Selecting more than 8 animals will show an error.
    • Once you have selected all the animals, click on the SUBMIT button.
